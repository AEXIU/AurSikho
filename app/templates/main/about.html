About Aursikho

Learn more about our mission and platform

Our Mission

Aursikho is an online course enrollment system built to make quality education accessible to everyone. Our platform enables instructors to create engaging courses with video lessons and quizzes, while students can enroll, learn at their own pace, and earn verifiable certificates upon completion.

How It Works

1
Enroll

Browse courses and enroll in topics that interest you — completely free.

2
Learn

Watch video lessons, read materials, and track your progress lesson by lesson.

3
Get Certified

Complete all lessons, pass the quiz, and download your verifiable certificate.

Technology Stack

Python Flask SQLite Jinja2 Bootstrap 5 Pillow

Certificate Verification

Every certificate issued by Aursikho comes with a unique verification ID. Anyone can verify the authenticity of a certificate using our verification page.
